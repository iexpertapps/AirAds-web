---

# **1️⃣ Package Philosophy (Value Ladder Strategy)**

AirAd’s monetization must align with its **“Nearby \+ Now”** philosophy and progressive activation strategy .

The value ladder is structured around **Visibility → Control → Automation → Dominance**.

| Tier | Strategic Position | Core Outcome |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Silver** | Entry Presence | Be visible |
| **Gold** | Competitive Edge | Be preferred |
| **Diamond** | Growth Engine | Convert efficiently |
| **Platinum** | Area Dominance | Own visibility |

The system must:

* Never block discovery (aligned with Phase-1 playbook)  
* Reward engagement, not just payment  
* Encourage upgrade based on measurable ROI  
* Scale without operational overhead

Monetization enhances visibility — it does not gate basic existence.

# **3️⃣ Usage Limits & Controls**

Designed for scalability and fairness.

### **Silver (Free / Entry)**

* Standard AR ranking  
* No scheduling  
* No voice automation  
* Limited reel refresh frequency  
* Basic analytics: views, taps

Purpose: Acquire vendors without friction.

---

### **Gold (Growth Tier)**

* Scheduled promotions  
* 1 Happy Hour/day  
* Basic voice intro (pre-recorded)  
* Moderate AR boost weight  
* Standard analytics (engagement \+ time slots)

Purpose: First meaningful upgrade trigger.

---

### **Diamond (Performance Tier)**

* Dynamic voice bot responses  
* 3 Happy Hours/day  
* Sponsored placement windows  
* Higher AR ranking multiplier  
* Advanced campaign scheduling  
* Conversion \+ time analytics

Purpose: Vendors who depend on daily volume.

---

### **Platinum (Dominance Tier)**

* Geo-zone AR dominance (landmark cluster priority)  
* Unlimited scheduling & promotions  
* Predictive slot recommendations (future-ready)  
* Voice bot priority routing  
* Featured in-area tag boosting (Layer 5 behavior tags)  
* Performance heatmap analytics

Purpose: High-density vendors & area leaders.

# **4️⃣ AR Visibility Weighting Logic**

AirAd ranking must remain trust-driven and scalable.

### **Core Ranking Formula (Simplified)**

Final Score \=  
(Intent Match × 0.30)  
\+ (Distance Weight × 0.25)  
\+ (Active Promotion × 0.15)  
\+ (Engagement Score × 0.15)  
\+ (Subscription Multiplier × 0.15)

### **Subscription Multipliers**

| Tier | Multiplier |
| ----- | ----- |
| Silver | 1.0 |
| Gold | 1.2 |
| Diamond | 1.5 |
| Platinum | 2.0 |

Important:

* Paid tier cannot override distance relevance completely.  
* Promotion tags (Layer 3\) still boost naturally .  
* ARPriority tag (Layer 5\) applied dynamically for Diamond+.

This protects user trust while monetizing visibility.

# **5️⃣ Monetization Psychology (Why Vendors Upgrade)**

### **1\. Fear of Invisibility**

When vendors see competitors marked:

* “Verified”  
* “Premium”  
* “Elite”  
* Appearing higher in AR

Upgrade pressure increases naturally.

---

### **2\. Time-Slot Monetization**

Micro-vendors think in:

* “Slow hours”  
* “Evening rush”  
* “Office lunch”

When they see:

* Gold vendors filling 3–5 PM via Happy Hour  
* Diamond vendors dominating lunch search

They upgrade to recover lost time.

---

### **3\. Voice Automation ROI**

Voice answers reduce friction:  
“Cheap pizza under 300 near me”

Diamond vendors:

* Appear in more voice results  
* Convert faster  
* Reduce manual answering

This becomes performance-driven monetization.

---

### **4\. AR Status Signaling**

In AR view:

* Silver → Plain marker  
* Gold → Verified badge  
* Diamond → Premium glow  
* Platinum → Crown \+ priority pin

Physical world \+ digital hierarchy \= strong psychological effect.

---

### **5\. Data Visibility**

When Silver sees:  
“Your listing had 240 views. Upgrade to see time breakdown.”

Curiosity drives conversion.

Analytics is one of the strongest upgrade levers.
