Creating Your Account

Getting started with AirAd is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Visit portal.airad.com and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  2. Enter your mobile phone number - this will be your primary login method

  4. Create a secure password for your account

Section 3 — Claiming Your Business

Why Claim Your Business?

Claiming your business verifies you as the legitimate owner and unlocks powerful features to manage your presence on AirAd. Only verified businesses can:

  • Edit business information and photos
  • Create promotional campaigns
  • Access customer analytics
  • Configure voice bot automation
  • Upgrade to premium subscription tiers

Step-by-Step Claim Process

Step 1: Start the Claim Process

From your dashboard, click the "Claim Your Business" button or navigate to the Profile section and select "Claim Business."

Step 2: Enable Location Detection

The portal will request access to your location to show nearby businesses. Click "Allow" when prompted by your browser. This helps us find businesses close to your current location.

Location Accuracy: For best results, enable location services while physically at your business location. This ensures the most accurate nearby listings.

Step 3: Find Your Business

You'll see a map and list view of businesses in your area. Use these tools to locate your business:

  • Map View: Browse businesses visually on the interactive map
  • List View: See all nearby businesses in a scrollable list
  • Search Bar: Type your business name or address for quick results
  • Filter Options: Filter by business category or distance

Step 4: Initiate the Claim

Once you find your business, click the "Claim This Business" button. You'll be asked to confirm this is the correct business before proceeding.

Step 5: OTP Verification

For security, we'll send a verification code to the business phone number on file. This ensures only legitimate business owners can claim listings.

  • Enter the 6-digit OTP code when you receive it
  • The code expires after 10 minutes
  • If you don't have access to the business phone, proceed to manual verification

Step 6: Manual Verification (Alternative)

If you can't receive OTP verification, you can verify manually by uploading documentation:

  • Click "Verify Manually" when OTP fails
  • Upload one of the following documents:
    • Business registration certificate
    • Utility bill with business address
    • Business license or permit
    • Tax registration document
  • Ensure documents are clear and show your business name and address
  • Submit for review by our verification team
Processing Time: Manual verification typically takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your claim is approved.

After Claim Approval

Once your claim is verified, you'll have full control over your business listing. You can immediately:

  • Edit business information, hours, and photos
  • Create your first promotional campaign
  • Access basic analytics and customer insights
  • Upgrade to premium subscription tiers
Congratulations! You're now ready to maximize your business visibility on AirAd. Start by completing your business profile to attract more customers.

Section 5 — Managing Your Profile

Your Business Profile is Your Digital Storefront

Your business profile is what customers see when they discover your business on AirAd. A complete, accurate profile builds trust and attracts more customers. This section covers everything you need to know about managing your business information.

Accessing Profile Management

To manage your business profile:

  1. Log into your Vendor Portal dashboard
  2. Click "Profile" in the left sidebar menu
  3. You'll see all editable sections of your business profile

Basic Business Information

Keep your core business details accurate and up-to-date:

  • Business Name: Your official business name as customers know it
  • Category: Select the primary category that best describes your business
  • Address: Complete physical address for accurate location mapping
  • Phone Number: Main business contact number
  • Website: Your business website URL (optional)
  • Email: Customer service email address
Accuracy Matters: Ensure your address is precise. Customers use GPS navigation to find your business, and incorrect addresses can lead to lost customers.

Business Hours

Set your operating hours to let customers know when you're available:

  • Click "Edit Business Hours" in your profile section
  • Set opening and closing times for each day of the week
  • Mark days when you're closed (holidays, maintenance, etc.)
  • Add special notes for irregular hours or seasonal changes
  • Click "Save" to update your hours immediately
Customer Experience: Outdated business hours frustrate customers. Update your hours promptly when they change, especially for holidays or special events.

Logo and Photos

Visual content helps customers recognize and trust your business:

  • Business Logo: Upload your official logo in high resolution
    • Recommended size: 400x400 pixels
    • Format: PNG or JPG
    • File size: Maximum 5MB
  • Business Photos: Upload multiple photos to showcase your business
    • Storefront/entrance photos
    • Interior shots showing ambiance
    • Product or service photos
    • Team photos (optional)
  • Drag to reorder photos - the first photo becomes your primary image
  • Click "Save Changes" to update your visual content
Photo Quality: Use bright, clear photos taken during business hours. Natural lighting works best. Avoid blurry images or photos with visible watermarks.

Business Description

Tell customers what makes your business special:

  • Write a compelling business description (maximum 500 characters)
  • Highlight what makes you unique
  • Mention your specialties or signature offerings
  • Include any awards or recognition
  • Use keywords customers might search for

Category Tags

Category tags help customers discover your business when browsing or searching:

  • Select up to 5 relevant category tags from the predefined list
  • Choose tags that accurately describe your products/services
  • Consider what customers might search for when looking for businesses like yours
  • Popular tags include: "family-friendly," "outdoor seating," "wifi," "delivery," etc.

Profile Completion Tips

A complete profile performs better in customer searches and builds more trust:

✅ Must-Have Elements
Accurate address, current hours, working phone number, business description
🌟 Trust Builders
Professional logo, quality photos, website link, customer reviews
🎯 Discovery Boosters
Relevant category tags, detailed description, special features highlighted
📱 Mobile Optimization
Ensure all information displays well on mobile devices
Profile Score: Your dashboard shows a profile completion percentage. Aim for 100% to maximize your visibility and customer trust.

Saving and Publishing Changes

When you make changes to your profile:

  • Click "Save Changes" after editing any section
  • Changes are published immediately to the AirAd platform
  • Customers will see updated information right away
  • You can edit your profile as often as needed
Review Before Publishing: Double-check all information for accuracy before saving, especially your address and phone number. Incorrect information can negatively impact customer experience.

Section 6 — Campaigns & Promotions

Drive Customer Engagement with Promotions

Promotional campaigns are powerful tools to attract new customers and increase repeat business. Available to Gold tier and above, these features help you stand out from competitors and drive immediate action from customers.

Accessing Campaign Management

To create and manage your promotional campaigns:

  1. Log into your Vendor Portal dashboard
  2. Click "Campaigns" in the left sidebar menu
  3. View your active campaigns and create new ones
Tier Requirement: Campaign features require Gold tier subscription or higher. Upgrade your plan to unlock these powerful marketing tools.

Discount Campaigns

Create compelling discounts that encourage customers to choose your business:

Creating a Discount Campaign

  1. Click "Create New Campaign" and select "Discount"
  2. Campaign Name: Give your discount a memorable title
  3. Discount Type: Choose percentage or fixed amount
    • Percentage: "20% off entire order"
    • Fixed amount: "$5 off minimum purchase of $25"
  4. Discount Value: Set the discount amount or percentage
  5. Minimum Purchase: Set a minimum order value (optional)
  6. Duration: Choose campaign start and end dates
  7. Description: Explain what the discount covers
  8. Click "Create Campaign" to publish

Best Practices for Discounts

💡 Effective Offers
10-25% off or $5-10 off works best for most businesses
⏰ Limited Time
Create urgency with 3-7 day campaign durations
📱 Clear Terms
Specify exactly what the discount applies to
🎯 Target Audience
Consider what appeals to your ideal customers

Happy Hour Promotions

Set up recurring happy hour promotions to drive traffic during slower periods:

  1. Click "Create New Campaign" and select "Happy Hour"
  2. Promotion Name: "Afternoon Special," "Evening Happy Hour," etc.
  3. Days of Week: Select which days the happy hour applies to
  4. Time Range: Set start and end times (e.g., 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
  5. Offer Details: Describe the special (e.g., "Buy one get one free drinks")
  6. Recurring Schedule: Set to repeat weekly or monthly
  7. Click "Create Happy Hour" to activate
Timing Strategy: Schedule happy hours during your typically slow periods to maximize their effectiveness and boost overall daily revenue.

Free Delivery Configuration

Attract more customers by offering free delivery within a specified area:

  1. Click "Create New Campaign" and select "Free Delivery"
  2. Delivery Radius: Set the distance for free delivery (e.g., 3 miles)
  3. Minimum Order: Set minimum purchase amount for free delivery
  4. Available Hours: Specify when free delivery is available
  5. Delivery Instructions: Add any special delivery notes for customers
  6. Click "Create Free Delivery Offer" to activate

Campaign Scheduling (Diamond+)

Diamond and Platinum tiers can schedule campaigns in advance:

  • Create campaigns up to 30 days in advance
  • Set exact start and end times down to the minute
  • Configure recurring campaigns for regular promotions
  • Batch create multiple campaigns at once

Monitoring Active Campaigns

Track the performance of your promotional campaigns:

  • Campaign Dashboard: View all active campaigns at a glance
  • Performance Metrics: Track views, taps, and conversion rates
  • Customer Engagement: See how many customers redeem offers
  • Revenue Impact: Monitor campaign ROI and revenue lift
  • A/B Testing: Compare different offers to see what works best
📊 Key Metrics
Views, taps, redemptions, revenue generated
🎯 Optimization
Use data to refine future campaign strategies

Managing Campaigns

Full control over your promotional campaigns:

  • Edit Campaigns: Modify details, extend duration, or update offers
  • Pause Campaigns: Temporarily halt campaigns without deleting them
  • End Campaigns: Stop campaigns before their scheduled end time
  • Duplicate Campaigns: Copy successful campaigns for quick setup
  • Delete Campaigns: Remove inactive campaigns from your dashboard

Campaign Compliance Guidelines

Ensure your promotions comply with platform policies:

  • All discounts must be authentic and redeemable
  • Clear terms and conditions for all offers
  • No misleading or false advertising
  • Honor all valid customer redemptions
  • Comply with local business regulations and laws
Policy Violations: Misleading promotions can result in campaign removal and account suspension. Always be transparent about your offers.
Success Strategy: Start with simple, attractive offers and track performance. Use the data to create increasingly effective campaigns that drive real business results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about using the AirAd Vendor Portal. These FAQs cover everything from getting started to advanced features.

1. How do I get started with AirAd?
Getting started is easy! Visit portal.airad.com, create an account with your phone number, and follow the step-by-step process to claim your business. The entire setup takes about 10-15 minutes.
2. Is AirAd really free to use?
Yes! AirAd offers a free Silver tier that includes basic business listing, profile management, and fundamental analytics. Premium features like promotional campaigns and voice bot automation require paid subscriptions starting at $29/month.
3. How does the AR visibility work?
When customers use the AirAd mobile app, they can see your business through their phone camera as a floating bubble in the real world. This makes your business impossible to miss and creates an engaging discovery experience.
4. What if I can't receive OTP verification for my business claim?
If you can't access the business phone number, you can use manual verification. Upload business documents like registration certificates, utility bills, or business licenses. Our team typically reviews these within 1-2 business days.
5. Can I change my subscription tier anytime?
Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades and cancellations take effect at your next billing cycle.
6. How do promotional campaigns work?
Create discounts, happy hours, or free delivery offers through the Campaigns section. Set your offer details, duration, and terms. Customers see these promotions when they discover your business and can redeem them directly.
7. What is Voice Bot and how does it help my business?
Voice Bot is an automated assistant that handles customer inquiries 24/7. It answers common questions about your business hours, location, services, and current promotions, reducing your workload while improving customer service.
8. How accurate are the analytics?
Our analytics provide real-time insights into customer behavior, including views, interactions, and campaign performance. Data is anonymized and aggregated to protect customer privacy while giving you actionable business intelligence.
9. Can I have multiple business locations?
Yes, you can manage multiple business locations from a single account. Each location needs to be claimed separately, and you can choose different subscription tiers for each location based on your needs.
10. How do I improve my AR visibility ranking?
Improve your ranking by completing your business profile, running promotional campaigns, upgrading your subscription tier, and maintaining regular activity. Higher-tier businesses get priority placement in AR views.
11. What happens if a customer has a bad experience with my business?
Customers can provide feedback through the app. Address issues promptly and professionally. Consistently poor customer experiences may affect your visibility ranking and could lead to account review.
12. Is my business data secure on AirAd?
Yes, we use industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Your data is protected and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
13. Can I export my business data?
Yes, you can export all your business data including analytics, customer interactions, and campaign performance at any time through your account settings. This ensures you always have access to your valuable business information.
14. What kind of support is available?
Silver tier gets email support, Gold tier adds priority email support, Diamond tier includes chat support, and Platinum tier provides priority phone support. Response times vary by tier from 24-48 hours for Silver to under 2 hours for Platinum.
15. How do I cancel my account?
You can cancel your subscription through account settings. Your subscription continues until the end of your billing period, after which you'll lose access to premium features. You can export your data before cancellation to keep your business information.

Technical Support FAQs

16. Why isn't my business appearing in AR?
Check that your address is accurate, your business is verified, and you have an active subscription. Also ensure your profile is complete and your business hours are current. Technical issues are rare but can be reported to support.
17. What browsers are supported for the Vendor Portal?
The Vendor Portal works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, keep your browser updated to the latest version and enable JavaScript.
18. How do I report bugs or technical issues?
Report technical issues through the support section of your account dashboard. Include details about your browser, device, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are helpful for faster resolution.

Billing and Payment FAQs

19. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards through our secure Stripe payment processor. Payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers. You can update your payment method anytime in account settings.
20. Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with the service, contact support within 30 days of your first payment for a full refund.
21. Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees! The price you see is the price you pay. All features included in your subscription tier are available at no additional cost. Optional add-ons or custom services are clearly priced before purchase.
